#VanRuby

We are from Vancouver and we build cool stuff with Ruby! We get together to work on personal projects, hack on open source and try to learn from each other.

Big Meetups

Once a month we have a big meetup and we have keynote speaker talk about something awesome in the Ruby community! This is a sweet event!

Hack Nights

First and third Tuesday of the month we get together to hack on cool stuff. All skill levels welcome!

Cool People

If you love Ruby or hacking on things, you're in good company! Vancouver has a bustling Ruby and open source community.
